😰 Feeling Stressed?

Stress is not always bad... it’s a signal that something needs attention. The key is to manage it, not let it control you.

😣 😫 💭 ⚡ 🌀

🤔💭 What You Can Do When You’re Stressed ? 💭🤔

  • Take slow, deep breaths to calm your body
  • Step away from work and take a short break
  • Go for a walk or stretch to release tension
  • Listen to calming music or sit quietly
  • Start with one small and easy task

😣 Things to Do When You're Stressed 😣

  • Identify the main cause of your stress
  • Break your tasks into smaller, manageable steps
  • Prioritize what really matters
  • Manage your time effectively
  • Ask for help if things feel overwhelming

❌⚠️ Things to Avoid ⚠️❌

  • Don’t try to do everything at once
  • Avoid overthinking and unnecessary pressure
  • Don’t ignore stress address it early
  • Avoid unhealthy coping habits

“Slow down. You don’t have to do everything at once”
